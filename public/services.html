Comprehensive Dental Services for Your Whole Family

At Affordable Dental of OKC, we combine advanced technology with a gentle, patient-first approach to deliver preventive, cosmetic, restorative, and minor surgical care. From routine checkups to full smile makeovers, every treatment is personalized for children, adults, and seniors across Oklahoma City, Norman, Newcastle, and surrounding areas.

Preventive Dentistry

Routine exams, cleanings, digital X-rays, and oral cancer screenings to keep your smile healthy year after year. We focus on early detection and gentle education so every visit feels comfortable and productive.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Professional whitening, veneers, and complete smile makeovers designed to boost your confidence. Whether you're preparing for a special occasion or simply want to love your reflection, our cosmetic solutions are tailored to you.

Restorative & Surgical Care

Fillings, crowns, bridges, root canals, dentures, periodontal treatment, and minor oral surgery. We restore function and aesthetics with precision, using the latest dental technology to ensure lasting results.

We proudly offer personalized treatment plans for every stage of life — from a child's first checkup to senior denture care. Our team is dedicated to making quality dentistry accessible and affordable for all Oklahoma families.
