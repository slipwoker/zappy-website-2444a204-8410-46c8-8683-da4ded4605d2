Complete Dental Care for the Whole Family

At Affordable Dental of OKC, we believe everyone deserves a healthy, confident smile. From preventive dentistry near me to advanced cosmetic and restorative treatments, our team provides gentle, personalized care using the latest technology. Whether you need a routine checkup or a full smile makeover, we're here for your family every step of the way.

Preventive Dentistry

Routine cleanings, exams, and oral cancer screenings to keep your smile healthy and catch issues early.

Teeth Whitening

Professional in-office and take-home whitening treatments to brighten your smile by several shades safely.

Veneers

Custom-crafted porcelain veneers for a natural-looking smile transformation and lasting confidence.

Crowns

Durable, tooth-colored crowns that restore strength, function, and aesthetics to damaged teeth.

Root Canals

Gentle, modern root canal therapy to relieve pain and save your natural tooth from extraction.

Fillings

Tooth-colored composite fillings that blend seamlessly and provide long-lasting protection against decay.

Dentures

Comfortable, natural-looking full and partial dentures designed to restore your smile and chewing function.

Periodontal & Oral Surgery

Gum disease treatment and minor surgical procedures to maintain healthy supporting structures for your teeth.

Meet Dr. Jason Chang

Since opening our doors in 2015, Affordable Dental and Orthodontics has been dedicated to providing exceptional dental care to families across Oklahoma City, Norman, Newcastle, and the surrounding communities.
